Resume Writing Tips from HR Professionals

Direct advice from hiring managers and HR specialists on creating resumes that get interviews.
Quantify Achievements
"Instead of 'Managed social media accounts,' write 'Grew Instagram following by 240% in 6 months through targeted campaigns.' Numbers get attention."
Tailor for Each Role
"We spot generic resumes immediately. Mention specific technologies from the job description and align your experience accordingly."
Optimize for ATS
"Avoid graphics, columns, and unusual fonts. We process thousands of resumes through tracking systems that read text only."
Focus on Impact
"Describe how your actions benefited previous employers. Did you save time? Increase revenue? Improve efficiency? That's what we care about."
